# AI Workflow

This document outlines the recommended approach for using AI tools within this project.

## Model Routing

*   **Local LM Studio:** For quick prototyping, small tasks, and private experimentation.
*   **OpenAI:** For architecture review, complex debugging, and when higher reasoning capabilities are needed.
*   **Anthropic:** Similar to OpenAI - use for critical reviews or when you need a different perspective.
*   **Mistral:**  For smaller tasks where Mistral’s performance is sufficient.

## Prompting Guidelines

*   Be specific with your prompts. Clearly state the task and desired output format.
*   Use examples to guide the AI's response.
*   Iterate on your prompts based on the AI's initial responses.
*   Always review the AI’s output carefully before applying it.
